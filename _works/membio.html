What is the Membio Platform?

Membio is a platform for members of the real estate industry to engage with each other and the associations that represent them.

It creates a stronger connection between associations and their members, and allows the members to gain real value from all the associatoins have to offer.

It also contains a marketing automation platform for associations to target members of the community to better help them reach their goals.

While at Membio, I was responsible for designing the entire member platform from the ground up, which included:

  1. User research studies with agents and association owners.
  2. Document user flows for engineering.
  3. Designing a progressive web app experience as well as a desktop version.
  4. Creating a design system for the entire Membio platform.
